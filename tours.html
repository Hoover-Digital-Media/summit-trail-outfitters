Our Tours

Every tour is led by a certified guide with deep knowledge of the trail, the ecosystem, and the conditions. Choose the adventure that fits your pace.

Day Hikes

Pine Ridge Loop

Moderate 4 hours Up to 10

A classic Pacific Northwest forest loop with panoramic ridge views and old-growth cedar groves. The trail climbs steadily for the first hour, then levels off along the ridge before descending through fern-lined switchbacks.

$65 per person

River Canyon Trail

Moderate 6 hours Up to 8

Follow the canyon rim above the Cascade River with two waterfall stops and a lunch break at a riverside overlook. This trail passes through three distinct ecosystems in a single day.

$85 per person

Full-Day Adventures

Summit Challenge

Strenuous Full day Up to 6

A demanding ascent to the high ridge — 2,800 feet of elevation gain over nine miles. The payoff is one of the most dramatic views in the Cascades. Participants should be in good physical condition and comfortable with sustained uphill hiking.

$120 per person
Book this tour

Wildflower Traverse

Strenuous Full day Up to 8

An early-season traverse across a high meadow basin known for its late-June wildflower bloom. The route includes a ridge crossing with views into two river valleys.

$110 per person
Book this tour

Overnight Trips

Alpine Lake Basecamp

Moderate Overnight Up to 6

Pack in to a high-alpine lake at 5,400 feet and set up camp on the shore. Your guide handles route-finding and camp setup while you take in the reflections, spot wildlife at dusk, and wake up above the clouds. All camping gear is provided.

$220 per person
Book this tour

Ridgeline Traverse

Strenuous Overnight Up to 4

A point-to-point overnight that crosses three named ridges and drops into a remote old-growth valley. Night one is spent at a backcountry shelter with a fire ring and stream access. Shuttle from the trailhead is included.

$275 per person
Book this tour
